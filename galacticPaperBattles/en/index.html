An unprecedented battle!

Get ready to face paper enemies in an unprecedented galactic battle! Each level has a powerful and unique boss, so always be ready for the final battle!


Unique Gameplay!

Destroy or dodge asteroids, colect items and unlock more powerful spaceships while you advance in the game! Colect and use the power-up items! They randomly appear in each level and may help you a lot! You can combine them to amplify their power!


Impossible!?

Can you finish the “impossible” difficulty mode? Enemies will shoot frenetically, asteroids will cause a massive damage and the bosses are more powerful than ever! Unlock the most powerful spaceships and face this challenge!
