Privacy Policy for MinersPal

At MinersPal, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when using the MinersPal app.

Information We Collect

When you use MinersPal, we may collect the following types of data:

  • Email Address
    We collect your email address when you create an account or communicate with us for support purposes. Your email is used solely for account management, app functionality, and communication purposes.
  • Miner Stats
    When you add miners to the app, we collect information about your mining setups, including miner name, public wallet address, and mining statistics. This data helps us provide real-time monitoring and improve your mining experience.
  • Community Interactions
    If you participate in the MinersPal Community, we may collect the content you post, such as messages, comments, or shared miner setups.

How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used to:

  • Provide and improve the MinersPal app’s functionality, including monitoring miner stats and allowing participation in the community.
  • Help you manage and track your mining performance.
  • Keep you informed with crypto-related news and updates.

Your Privacy Matters

  • No Tracking: MinersPal does not track your activity within the app or across other apps or websites.
  • No Personal Association: Any data collected, such as miner stats or community interactions, is not linked to any personally identifiable information beyond your email address, which is used solely for account-related purposes.
  • Anonymous Usage: Where possible, we ensure that your mining data is anonymized and not associated with personal details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties without your consent. However, you have the option to share your miner setups with other users within the MinersPal community. If you choose to do so, only the miner stats you provide will be visible to other users—your personal identity will remain private.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask for any incorrect data to be corrected.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data, subject to certain legal obligations.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here, and we encourage you to review it periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, feel free to contact us at info@bitpal-miners.com.
